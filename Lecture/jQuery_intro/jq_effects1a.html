Below is a partial list of the core/main jQuery "actions". Let's script this page so that:
  • When the page loads it hide all dd objects.
  • Bind a mouseover onto all dt objects that does an addClass on $(this) for the "highlight" class.
  • Then add a bind of a mouseout onto all dt objects that does a removeClass on $(this) for the "highlight" class (to remove/undo the above change).
  • Take specific ID pairs, such as #dtaddclass and #ddaddclass and bind a "click" to the dt, that does a toggle or slideToggle on the corresponding dd.
  • Take another pair and do a toggle().
  • Take another pair and do a fadeToggle().
